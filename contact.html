    Before You Reach Out

    📋 How to Prepare for an Estimate

    • Know your approximate project dimensions (length × width)
    • Decide on the type of addition or improvement you need
    • Have a rough budget range in mind
    • Note any special requirements (permits, HOA rules, accessibility)
    • Gather photos or plans if available — attach them above

    📬 What to Expect Next

    1. Confirmation: We'll confirm receipt of your message within a few hours.
