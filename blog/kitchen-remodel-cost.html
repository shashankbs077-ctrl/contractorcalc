Kitchen Remodel Cost Guide: Where to Spend and Save

7 min read · Remodeling

The kitchen is the most expensive room to remodel — and the one with the highest return on investment. A mid-range kitchen remodel recoups about 70–80% of its cost in home value. Here's how to spend your kitchen budget wisely.

Kitchen Remodel Cost Ranges

Remodel TypeAverage CostROI
Minor refresh (paint, hardware, lighting)$5,000–$15,00080–90%
Mid-range remodel$25,000–$50,00070–80%
Major renovation$50,000–$100,00060–70%
Luxury custom kitchen$100,000+50–60%

Pro Tip: Cabinet refacing (replacing doors and drawer fronts while keeping the boxes) costs 30–50% less than full cabinet replacement and can make an older kitchen look completely new.

Biggest Mistakes to Avoid

  • Moving the sink or changing the kitchen layout — adds thousands in plumbing costs
  • Buying appliances last — choose them first since cabinets are built around them
  • Skipping the permit — unpermitted work can cause issues when selling
  • Underestimating the timeline — kitchen remodels typically take 4–8 weeks
