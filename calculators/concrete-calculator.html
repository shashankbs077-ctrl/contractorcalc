Frequently Asked Questions

For a 10×10 foot slab that's 4 inches thick, you'll need approximately 1.23 cubic yards or 56 bags of 80-lb concrete mix including 10% waste factor.
A cubic yard of wet concrete weighs approximately 3,900-4,100 pounds (about 2 tons).
Use bagged concrete for projects under 1 cubic yard. Ready-mix is more economical for projects over 2 cubic yards.
Standard thicknesses: 4 inches for patios and walkways, 5-6 inches for residential driveways, 6-8 inches for heavy vehicle driveways.
Concrete is walkable after 24-48 hours. It reaches 70% strength in 7 days and full strength in 28 days. Keep concrete moist during the first week for proper curing.
