Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our calculators and estimates.

Are these calculators really free? +
Yes, all 20 calculators are 100% free to use with no signup required. We support our website through non-intrusive advertising, which allows us to provide professional-grade tools at no cost to you. There are no hidden paywalls, premium tiers, or "pro" versions—every feature is available to everyone.
How accurate are the calculations? +
Our calculators use industry-standard formulas that professional contractors rely on. Material quantities are highly accurate when you input correct measurements. Cost estimates are based on national averages and should be used as guidelines—actual prices vary by location, supplier, and market conditions. We recommend getting quotes from local suppliers for final pricing.
Can I use these for professional contractor work? +
Absolutely! Many contractors use our calculators for quick estimates on job sites. The formulas are the same ones used in professional estimating software. You can print or download results to share with clients. However, for formal bids and contracts, we recommend verifying calculations and using your local material pricing.
Why do you include a waste factor? +
Every construction project requires extra material to account for cutting waste, breakage, mistakes, and future repairs. Industry standards typically recommend 5-15% extra depending on the material and project complexity. Our calculators include adjustable waste factors so you can customize based on your experience level and project requirements.
How often are material costs updated? +
We update our cost data regularly to reflect current market conditions. However, material prices can vary significantly by region, supplier, and season. The costs shown are national averages intended to help with budgeting and comparison. Always verify prices with your local suppliers for accurate project budgeting.
Do you save my calculation data? +
No, we do not store any of your calculation inputs or results on our servers. All calculations happen directly in your browser. If you want to save results, use the print or download features. We respect your privacy and do not collect personal project information. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
